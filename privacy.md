# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 13, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you interact with our WhatsApp Business messaging service (the "Service").

**The Service is operated by an automated AI assistant.** Messages you send are processed by artificial intelligence systems, not exclusively by human agents. Please read Section 4 for details on how automated processing works and how you can reach a human.

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **WhatsApp phone number** — the number you use to message us
- **WhatsApp profile name** — as made available by WhatsApp
- **Message content** — text, images, documents, and other media you send to us
- **Any information you voluntarily include** in your messages

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Message metadata** — timestamps, message IDs, delivery and read status
- **Conversation history** — prior messages in your conversation, retained to give the AI assistant context for follow-up replies
- **Technical logs** — request records used for debugging and service reliability

We do **not** collect your WhatsApp contacts, chat history with other parties, location data, or payment card information.

### 2.3 Please Do Not Send Sensitive Information

Because your messages are processed by automated systems and transmitted to third-party AI providers (Section 5), **please do not send sensitive personal information** through this Service — including government ID numbers, financial account or card numbers, passwords, health information, or biometric data.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

- Generate automated replies to your messages using AI systems
- Deliver, respond to, and manage messages you send us
- Provide customer support and service-related notifications
- Maintain, secure, monitor, and improve the quality and reliability of the Service
- Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** use your information for advertising or third-party marketing.

## 4. Automated Processing and AI Disclosure

### 4.1 You Are Interacting With an AI Assistant

Replies you receive are generated by an automated AI system. The assistant is identified as such at the start of each conversation.

### 4.2 How Automated Processing Works

When you send a message, the message content and recent conversation history are transmitted to a third-party AI model provider (see Section 5), which generates a response. That response is returned to you via WhatsApp.

### 4.3 Human Review

Human staff may review conversations for quality assurance, safety, troubleshooting, and handling escalations. Access is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.

### 4.4 Requesting a Human

You may request a human agent at any time by replying **HUMAN** (or **人工**). We will route your conversation to a person as soon as one is available.

### 4.5 Automated Decision-Making

The Service does **not** make decisions that produce legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affect you on a solely automated basis. If this changes, we will update this policy. Where applicable law (including Article 22 GDPR) grants you the right to obtain human intervention, express your point of view, and contest a decision, you may exercise that right by contacting us (Section 13).

### 4.6 Accuracy Limitations

AI-generated responses may be inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date. Responses are provided for informational and support purposes only and should not be relied upon as professional advice. Verify anything important independently.

### 4.7 Model Training

Your messages are **not** used to train or fine-tune AI models — neither by us nor, under our contractual arrangements, by our AI model providers.
