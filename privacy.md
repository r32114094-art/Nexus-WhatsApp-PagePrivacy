# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 13, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you interact with our services, including our WhatsApp Business messaging service (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **WhatsApp phone number** — the number you use to message us
- **WhatsApp profile name** — as made available by WhatsApp
- **Message content** — text, images, documents, and other media you send to us
- **Any information you voluntarily include** in your messages

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Message metadata** — timestamps, message IDs, delivery and read status
- **Technical logs** — request records used for debugging and service reliability

We do **not** collect your WhatsApp contacts, chat history with other parties, location data, or payment card information.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

- Deliver, respond to, and manage messages you send us
- Provide customer support and service-related notifications
- Maintain, secure, and improve the reliability of the Service
- Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** use your information for advertising or third-party marketing.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable law requires a legal basis for processing (e.g., GDPR), we rely on:

- **Consent** — you initiate contact with us via WhatsApp
- **Performance of a contract** — to provide the service you requested
- **Legitimate interests** — to secure and improve the Service
- **Legal obligation** — where retention or disclosure is required by law

## 5. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- **Meta Platforms, Inc. / WhatsApp** — as the infrastructure provider for message delivery. Their handling of data is governed by the [WhatsApp Privacy Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy).
- **Service providers** — hosting and infrastructure vendors acting under contract on our behalf, bound by confidentiality obligations.
- **Legal requirements** — where disclosure is required by law, regulation, legal process, or a valid governmental request.

We do not otherwise disclose your information to third parties.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain message content and associated metadata only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, and no longer than **12 months**, unless a longer period is required by law.

You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Section 8).

## 7. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and restricted internal access on a need-to-know basis.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 12. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30 days).
